Key Growth Opportunities

6 Levers That Will Move
the Needle

1
Identity Carousels — "For the mom who…"
Drives saves + shares at scale. Zero carousels posted to date = completely untapped engine. Each carousel slide should feel like turning a page in a simple story. Cover = identity hook. Final slide = faith encouragement.
2
Stories — The Relationship Bridge
Reels bring strangers in. Stories make them stay. Zero Stories posted = no warm community whatsoever. Starting Stories today converts your 95.5% cold-reach followers into loyal regulars. Polls, behind-the-scenes, honest check-ins.
3
Lead Magnet + Email List — The Owned Audience
236 profile visits, only 2 link taps (0.8%). A free PDF — "5 Things Nobody Tells You About Losing Yourself in Motherhood" — captures emails and owns the relationship outside Instagram. Set up on Flodesk or ConvertKit free tier.
4
Comment Bait Posts — Algorithm Priority
6 total comments in 89 days is the weakest metric on the account. Instagram 2026 weights comment velocity heavily. One direct question per post — "Drop a 🤍 if you've cried in the car this week" — can 10× comment volume with zero extra effort.
5
Trial Reels — Low-Risk Testing
Instagram's 2026 Trial Reels feature shows content to non-followers first before your existing audience sees it. Test new hooks without risking your current community's trust. Ideal for experimenting with content that feels different from your current tone.
6
Seasonal Viral Moments
Mother's Day, back-to-school, New Year devotional content spikes reach in the faith-mom niche annually without fail. Each is a planned virality window. Your Sept back-to-school push and Dec New Year reset series are key momentum builders for the 10K push.
